Dominating Amazon Search: A Guide for Sellers

"On Amazon, if you aren't on the first page, you don't exist. The key is knowing what shoppers type when they have their credit card out."

The A10 Algorithm vs. Traditional SEO

Amazon's search engine is built with one goal: conversion. Unlike Google, which prioritizes information, Amazon prioritizes sales velocity and relevance. To win, you need to align your listing with the specific "buyer intent" keywords shoppers are using right now.

Mastering "Backend" Keywords

Amazon provides a hidden field for backend search terms. This is where you put high-volume variations that didn't make it into your public-facing title. RankKV's Amazon platform filter allows you to see the exact search volumes for these terms, ensuring you utilize every single byte of that 250-character limit.

  • No commas or repetition needed
  • Include common misspellings
  • Prioritize long-tail buyer phrases

Keyword Research for PPC & Organic

Your organic strategy should focus on keywords where you can maintain a high conversion rate. Use RankKV to find keywords with "Transactional" intent.
